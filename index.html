Organic, Single-Origin, Bean-to-Bar Chocolate

Made in small batches directly from responsibly-sourced cacao beans. Honest ingredients and keeping it simple, as the Olmec and Mayan people would have done over 2000 years ago.

Eco-friendly production and packaging

We aim to keep the chocolate as sustainable as possible. The chocolate factory used to make it is off-grid and solar-powered, and we only use sustainable and biodegradable packaging. The beans are bought in bulk and shipped by sea.
